- A shade has a base fabric and lining but could incorporate 1 to n other fabrics (bestline or COM)

- A shade may or may not include a valance.  It is basically a separate item but in FMP it is sorta integrated.

- A fabric has width, repeat and price per ft values.

- Should be able to spawn notes on certain fields/layouts.  These fields/layouts may be specific to certain options/fabrics/styles and should be available or not dynamically.

- Styles and options have individual price values that make up the whole of the shade price.  These values come from shadeSpecs and optionSpecs.

- Shades have associated shapes.  This is a future epic.
